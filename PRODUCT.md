# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

- **Local families** from Ankleshwar & nearby towns looking for a premium pure-vegetarian dining experience for everyday meals and family celebrations
- **Couples & event planners** seeking a banquet hall for weddings, receptions, engagements, and milestone parties
- **Corporate clients** in GIDC Ankleshwar needing venue space for business events, conferences, and team gatherings
- **Online searchers** comparing Ankleshwar restaurants on Justdial / Google / Restaurant Guru; ratings-driven decision makers
- **Takeaway & delivery customers** wanting quality vegetarian meals at home

## Product Purpose

La Festiva Restaurant & Banquet exists to be Ankleshwar's premier pure-vegetarian dining and celebration destination. The website's job: convert visitors into diners and event bookings by communicating the restaurant's premium quality, warm ambiance, and banquet capabilities. Success means phone/WhatsApp inquiries, foot traffic from new customers, and banquet event reservations.

## Brand Personality

Warm, elegant, celebratory, family-rooted. Three words: **Premium · Warm · Celebratory**

The brand speaks with genuine warmth and quiet confidence. Not flashy or trendy — timeless, refined, and trustworthy. The voice is gracious host, not hype salesman.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI-generated website look (purple gradients, Inter-only typography, nested card-in-card layouts)
- Overly modern/minimal SaaS-style design (cold, uninviting for a restaurant)
- Bounce animations, elastic transitions, or excessive motion effects
- Pure black (#000) or pure gray (#808080) color usage — the brand is warm, not industrial
- Loud, cluttered layouts with competing calls-to-action
- Dark patterns like hidden pricing or aggressive pop-ups

## Design Principles

1. **Premium restraint.** Fewer elements, better quality. Every section earns its place. White space is a feature, not an absence.
2. **Warmth through depth, not decoration.** Rich burgundy and gold carry the warmth; textures, subtle patterns, and lighting effects add depth without clutter.
3. **Celebration-first.** Every page leads toward action: booking a table, ordering food, reserving the banquet hall. The site is a gateway to real-world hospitality.
4. **Typography as atmosphere.** Cormorant Garamond for display headings conveys elegance and tradition; Inter for body keeps the experience grounded and readable. The contrast is deliberate.
5. **Mobile-before-elegance.** The site must feel equally premium on a phone as on desktop. No compromise on small screens.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum (contrast ≥4.5:1 for body text)
- Reduced motion support via `prefers-reduced-motion`
- Semantic HTML structure throughout
- Touch-friendly tap targets (minimum 44px)
- Readable font sizes (no text below 14px on mobile)
